Ken Wakui

Creator of Tokyo Revengers

After being fired from his first job, Wakui often spent time with street gangs. However, he later found work as a bar host and eventually graduated from high school. In 2005, Wakui's Shinjuku Swan began serialization in Weekly Young Magazine where it ran until 2013.
